What Happens If My Flight Is Not Activated

+

If a flight pending activation has not been activated before 7 days of the departure, passengers will be invited to choose another flight or they can also choose to receive a full refund of their deposit.

What is an Activated Flight

+

Activated: The flight is confirmed and the aircraft is assigned to perform the flight. You will receive the flight briefing with information about the aircraft, boarding details and any additional information for your flight within 24 h after booking. You just need to start packing and get ready for your flight.

How Do I Propose A shared flight

+

If you want to start your own shared flight, you will find more information about the possibilities via our start a shared flight page.

Who Can Book A Flight?

+

The leading client (the one purchasing the tickets) must be, at least, 18 years old. Minors (passengers under 18 years old, by the time of the flight) can only fly accompanied by their parents or legal guardian. Unaccompanied minors, unfortunately, are not accepted on our shared flights. Passengers must be at least 2 years old on the date of the flight. Unfortunately, infants are not accepted on our shared flight Seat allocation can only be purchased via Hyer.

How Much Does It Cost

+

The price depends on the total price for chartering the aircraft, which varies according to route and date of the flight, as well as, how many spare seats are available for jet sharing. The latest prices are always available online.
